VIDYA FOUNDATION


Established in 2007, VIDYA FOUNDATION is a non-profit that focusses on mobilising communities for girls’ education in

India’s rural and educationally backward areas.Strongly aligned with the ‘Right to Education Act’ or the ‘Samagra Siksha’,

VIDYA FOUNDATION is committed to the Government’s vision to improve access to primary education for children, especially young girls.

VIDYA FOUNDATION currently operates successfully in over 18,000+ villages in Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.

By leveraging the Government’s existing investment in schools and by engaging with a huge base of community volunteers,

VIDYA FOUNDATION helps to identify, enrol and retain out-of-school girls and to improve foundational skills in literacy and numeracy

for all children (both girls and boys). This helps deliver measurable results to a large number of children and avoids

parallel delivery of services.
